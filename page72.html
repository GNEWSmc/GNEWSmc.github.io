Mom, who Threw Her Newborn Child in a Crib, was Imprisoned

By Inquirer.net posted December 15, 2019

A mom, who says she was upset with her newborn baby, threw the child to a hospital crib in order to stop him from crying.
Megan James, the newborn's mother, is charged with child endangerment and was imprisoned in Iowa, USA, on a $30,000 (P1.5 million) bail, as per Fox affiliate, KTIV last Friday, Dec. 13.
According to court documents, the 32-two-year-old woman gave birth to a healthy boy at Sioux City hospital on November 15. Early on, the baby's injuries made the hospital staff suspicious, announced on the same day local radio station KSCJ.
Finally, the mother admitted that she was wrong. She said that while she tried to breastfeed him, she felt exasperated with the boy, according to KTIV. She was telling the police
When his head hit the crib and the cement, the study said, the child sustained bilateral skull fractures. He was referred with his wounds to the Children's Hospital in Omaha.

Fatal Telephone Signal Search: Brgy. President, Teenager Killed; 1 Wounded in the Eastern Ambush of Negros

By Florence Baesa posted December 15, 2019

Searching for an area to get a mobile phone signal in Barangay Kalamtukan City, Bayawan City, Negros Oriental proved to be fatal to a barangay captain and a 16-year-old boy after several armed men sprayed the barangay rescue vehicle on at 7:30 p.m. Beginning 14 December 2019.
Johnny Condez, Kalamtukan's 47-year-old barangay captain, and 16-year-old Danny Marata Jr., were killed in the evening ambush in a statement provided by the Negros Oriental Police Office (NOPPO) this morning (December 15).
Danny Marata Sr., 48 years old and the father of the child, was wounded in the attack while another 52-year-old friend, Edilito Magbanua, survived the gunmen's bullet hail.
Initial investigation found that, together with two escorts riding two motorcycles, the four victims were on board a barangay rescue vehicle and heading for an elevated area in the barangay to look for a mobile phone signal. As they shot multiple times at the victims, the attackers were only four to six meters away from their target.
Fifteen empty shells of 5.56 caliber rifle, two slugs of a 12-gage shotgun, an empty shell of a 12-gage shotgun, four empty shells of a.45 caliber pistol, three 9 mm Luger pistol fired bullet, a M16 Armalite sniper magazine with 30 rounds of ammunition and a.45 caliber pistol slug were found at the crime scene.

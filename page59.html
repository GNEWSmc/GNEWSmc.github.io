6.9 Magnitude Earthquake in Padada

By Inquirer.net posted December 15, 2019

The Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology (Phivolcs) said on Sunday, a strong 6.9 earthquake shook Padada, Davao del Sur. Phivolcs said the tremor was tectonic and centered at a depth of 30 kilometres, sending people out of their homes and buildings in Digos City. The earthquake struck at 2:11 p.m. The epicenter is 6 kilometers northwest of Padada.
Residents in the area may expect further damage and shocks, Phivolcs added.
In October, a string of powerful quakes struck Mindanao, leaving several dead, dozens injured, and houses and buildings destroyed or damaged.
